# Daily Sales Trend Analysis

## Objective

The objective of this analysis was to understand the historical sales behaviour of Walmart before developing forecasting models.

## Observations

### Overall Trend

The total daily sales show an overall increasing trend between 2011 and 2016.

This indicates long-term growth in customer demand.

---

### Seasonal Patterns

The sales graph contains repeated peaks and dips throughout the time period.

This suggests the presence of seasonal demand patterns that should be considered during forecasting.

---

### Sudden Sales Drops

Several days contain extremely low sales values.

These observations will be investigated further using the calendar dataset to determine whether they correspond to holidays, store closures, or other special events.

---

### Sales Variability

Daily sales fluctuate considerably over time.

This indicates that demand is influenced by multiple business factors such as promotions, events, pricing, and seasonality.

---
